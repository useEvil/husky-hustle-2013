Welcome to Hicks Canyon Husky Hustle

{% include "include/thermometer.html" %}

Weclome to the 5th annual Husky Hustle Jog-a-thon Fundraiser. Last year we raised over $20,000 in donations and we're upping our goal to $30,000 this year. One change we're making this year is to go paperless (or at least partially) and accept Online Payments. Parents and Sponsors will now be able to make all their donations online. We're hoping this will simplify the process for sponsors to donate and make collecting funds easier for parents.

there are 2 ways to sponor your child:

Flat Amount:

Make a one-time donation in a flat amount regardless of how many laps your child runs. In addition, students and their families can also sponsor ANY Hicks teachers and/or Mrs. Agopian by making a flat amount donation. There is a separate section on the Pledge Sheet to itemize Teacher/Principal pledges. Flat amount donations should be turned in with this envelope on March 7.

Per Lap:

Pledge to pay an amount for each lap your child runs. The total laps will be counted and recorded during the Jog-a-thon. The amount due for each pledge will be calculated and entered on the pledge sheet and sent home with your child to collect from the sponsors. Per lap pledges and any other amounts due are to be turned in during the week of March 11-15.

Every student is to turn in their Pledge Envelope to their teacher on Thursday, March 7, (the day before the Jog-a-thon), whether or not the student has collected pledges or received money.

Cash or checks payable to Hicks Canyon PTO or Online Paymets. Online Payments are preferred, please encourage your sponsors to make payments online. If you would like to write a single check to cover all donations, please do!

After your child runs, we will calculate any amount due and the envelope will be returned to you. Please send in any outstanding pledges by Friday, March 15.

Prizes will be awarded to top runners and top money raisers on Friday, April 19.

Volunteers:

We always welcome volunteers. Parents if you think you can help, please contact us and let us know. We need volunteers for water duty and lap counting.
