  • Instructions as a Parents:
    • Parent Registration:
      • A. Parents can register on the Husky Hustle Website using the "Login / Register" menu option.
        1. From the Home Page of www.huskyhustle.com click on "Login / Register" option
        2. Click the "Not Registered?" link
        3. Provide all required fields and click "Register" to submit
        4. Wait for "Activation Link" to arrive in your email inbox
        5. Click the "Activation Link" to activate your account
      • B. Parents can also register with their social logins for "Facebook" , "Twitter" or "Google".
        1. Click the social network you would like to use for logging in
        2. You will be presented with an authorization page asking for you to authorize the Husky Hustle Website
        3. Accept the authorization
        4. Once redirected back to the Husky Hustle Website, provide registration information and submit
        5. Your account will be automatically activated
    • Registering Your Student:
      1. Login to the Husky Hustle Website
      2. Go to the "Profile" section, if not already there
      3. Scroll to the "Add a Student" section
      4. Provide information for your Student
      5. If a student has already been registered by a different parent, you can have your account linked with the other parent.
      6. Linking requires that the other parent accept the request to link accounts.
    • Adding Your Sponsors:
      1. Login to the Husky Hustle Website
      2. Go to the "Profile" section, if not already there
      3. Click on the Student's name
      4. Click on "Add Sponsor"
      5. Fill out the form with your Sponsor's information
      6. If you made a flat donation, you can make your payment on the "Thank You" page by clicking the "Make your Payment" link
    • Make Donation to a Teacher:
      1. Login to the Husky Hustle Website
      2. Go to the "Profile" section, if not already there
      3. Click on the Student's name
      4. Click on "Add Sponsor"
      5. Scroll down to the "Make a Donation to a Teacher" link and click it
      6. Select the Teacher you would like to donate to, select a donation amount or add your own amount and submit
      7. You can make your payment on the "Thank You" page by clicking the "Make your Payment" link
  • Instructions as a Sponsor:
    • Sponsoring a Student:
      1. Search for the Student or Parent of the Student you would like to Sponsor
      2. Select the Student from the list or results by clicking on the Student's name
      3. Provide your information, Donation amount and submit
      4. If you made a flat donation, you can make your payment on the "Thank You" page by clicking the "Make your Payment" link
    • If Student CAN NOT be found:
      1. Go to http://www.huskyhustle.com/make-donation/
      2. Provide the Student's name and their Teacher
      3. Provide your information, Donation amount and submit
      4. If you made a flat donation, you can make your payment on the "Thank You" page by clicking the "Make your Payment" link
    • Making a Donation to a Teacher for a Student:
      1. Search for the Student or Parent of the Student you would like to Sponsor
      2. Select the Student from the list or results by clicking on the Student's name
      3. Scroll down to the "Make a Donation to a Teacher" link and click it
      4. Select the Teacher you would like to donate to, select a donation amount or add your own amount and submit
      5. You can make your payment on the "Thank You" page by clicking the "Make your Payment" link
    • If Student CAN NOT be found:
      1. Go to http://www.huskyhustle.com/teacher-donation/
      2. Provide the Student's name and their Teacher
      3. Select the Teacher you would like to donate to, select a donation amount or add your own amount and submit
      4. You can make your payment on the "Thank You" page by clicking the "Make your Payment" link
  • Making Donations Directly with PayPal:
    • Donating Directly to a Student:
      1. From the Home Page of www.huskyhustle.com click on the "Donation" button
      2. You will be redirected to PayPal's payment page
      3. Fill out the donation amount
      4. Login with your PayPal account or continue without one
      5. Fill in your payment information and submit to "Review" your payment
      6. Click on the "Notes" link to specify the Student's name and Teacher
      7. Submit to make your Donation
    • Making a Donation Directly to a Teacher:
      1. From the Home Page of www.huskyhustle.com click on the "Donation" button
      2. You will be redirected to PayPal's payment page
      3. Fill out the donation amount
      4. Login with your PayPal account or continue without one
      5. Fill in your payment information and submit to "Review" your payment
      6. Click on the "Notes" link to specify the Teacher's name and/or Student's name
      7. Submit to make your Donation
